Rachel FRASER

About the Artist

Rachel Fraser (She/They) is a multi practice and interdisciplinary artist working across digital media, live performance and visual art.

With a HND in Acting and Performance and a BA in Dance and Drama, they are a trained performer, maker and fasciliator. Since graduating in 2021 they have been collaborating with Moot Point Collective as an animator and director, as well as focusing on thier own playwrighting and making.

Headshot by Josie Young Photography


Photography by Joseph Redpath

Recent work includes directing a rehearsed reading of the brand new play 'Kneecaps' (2021) by Joe Hunter, animating the short films 'towel' (2020) and 'Seperate' (2021), performing in the dance-comedy 'Simply Absurd' (2021) by Emily Briggs and co-creating and performing in 'Splitting at the Seems: The Story of Seemingly Theatre' (2021) a mockumentary created with fellow BA graduates.
